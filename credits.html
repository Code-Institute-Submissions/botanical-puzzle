This project was inspired by Food For Free (Mabey, 2007, Harper Collins). All illustrations included in the project are taken from this publication.

Credits are given to the following artists:

Marjorie Blamey, Jill Coombs, Victoria Goaman, Sheila Hadley, Marilyn Leader, David More, Valerie Price, Susannah Ray, Fiona Silver, Sue Wickison, John Wilkinson.


Whilst some of the items in this game are representations of real wild food it is NOT advised that you use this game as a plant identifier, as some of them may easily be mistaken for poisonous plants.

If you are interested in foraging or learning more, Food For Free can be purchased online.
